Who We Are

Hope-filled worship, vibrant ministries, and living faith.

We are a Christ-centered community serving the greater Atlanta area through uplifting worship services, discipleship, and ministry. Our fellowship celebrates Ghanaian heritage while welcoming everyone to belong, grow, and serve.

Weekly gatherings include Sabbath School, Divine Worship, youth programming, and midweek prayer. Beyond the sanctuary, we support families, provide outreach, and equip members to live missionally.
